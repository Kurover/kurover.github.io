Welcome

"4k art? Sorry, best I could do is around 128x^2"

Hello, this is Zan, also known as Kurover. I'm a pixel artist who mainly draw art and animation from Touhou Project.

In here, I have set you up for a quick overview about the creation I've made.. once I actually built them of course.

Back then,

Art was once just a hobby of mine since 2013. It all started by me, innocently, scrolling through Facebook timeline and saw one of my, well, "friend of a friend", drew this amazing piece of anime art. I wondered to myself, that it would be cool if I also could do the thing whatever they're doing.

With 1 art piece per month ratio after a year of training since then, we jump 5 revolution later into the year 2017. There, I dipped my flimsy little toes into pixel art and it was the most fun experience I've ever had in the world artistry.

Now we are here. Trying a bit into writing, while also keeping up with the drawing. Oh, I guess HTML/CSS too, eh? Oh uh, photography and video editing? Err.. doll making- ok I guess I do have a fair share of problems in my hand.
